ABOUT

Hello! I'm

Marcos Paulo Coelho

Ever since I was young, I've been passionate about art and drawing. This passion led me to get a degree in architecture, where I discovered my love for 3D art. I became fascinated with it and dedicated myself to learning and refining my skills independently.

Today, I'm a 3D Generalist Artist and Architect specializing in environment design and 3D visualization. My work involves lighting, texturing, animation, and post-production. I take pride in bringing creativity and precision to every project, turning ideas into immersive digital realities.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I enjoy creating it.
